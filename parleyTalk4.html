IF THE OCEANS DIE, WE DIE
We need to defend diversity on land and in the sea and we need solutions, and these solutions can only be realized by harnessing the imaginative side of human culture, THE ARTS
Parsons hosts its first Parley Talk on April 19, as part of The New School's Earth Week events series. This talk features guest speakers TBD and explores the role of artists, fashion designers, filmmakers, and product designers in reshaping the current reality of our oceans.
Parley believes artists, musicians, actors, filmmakers, fashion designers, journalists, architects, product inventors, and scientists have the tools to mold the reality we live in and to develop alternative business models and ecologically sensible products to give us earthlings an alternative choice, an everyday option to change something.
PARLEY IS THE SPACE WHERE CREATORS, THINKERS AND LEADERS COME TOGETHER TO RAISE AWARENESS FOR THE BEAUTY AND FRAGILITY OF OUR OCEANS AND COLLABORATE ON PROJECTS THAT CAN END THEIR DESTRUCTION.
Tuesday, April 19, 2016 John L. Tishman Auditorium, University Center 63 Fifth Avenue, Room U100, New York, NY 10003
